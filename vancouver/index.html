You don't have to have an idea to join us. We're a social group who tries to help each other make our ideas happen. You can be any age, ethnicity, and identify as any gender, you just have to be willing to contribute constructively.

New to Vancouver? We'e a great place to meet fellow creative and innovative folks. Our meetings average 10-15 people, however we've had some meetings be over 30.

We mostly use the Facebook Page and Vancouver Founders meetup.com group to announce events.

When you're at the next meeting, be sure to ask to be added to the members Slack/chat group.

Minutes:

Minutes are taken and published by volunteers at each of the ideas meetings. If you would like to take the minutes for one of the meetings the following guide provides a set of basic instructions.
