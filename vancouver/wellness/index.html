The Vancouver Data Driven Wellness Meetup meets on the second Thursday of the month from 7-10pm at Creative Coworkers. [MAP]

We have a meetup.com group .

If you're interested in wellness; including nutrition, mindfulness, values, and self-awareness, and want to improve these things for yourself and for the community from a data driven perspective, welcome.

We will be drawing from already-published and scrutinized data/conclusions, and then specifically how a Vancouverite can take action (including through products/services/groups) as well as formulating new hypotheses and tests and doing measurements here in Vancouver.

This is a collaborative group, and the only criticisms invited should be constructive.

When you're at the next meeting, be sure to ask to be added to the members Slack/chat group.
